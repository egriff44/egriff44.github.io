Hardcore Hardware


Art and animation work done as a part of a beginner indie game jam! The game theme was "Outside the Boundaries". We had a team of 4 (2 devs, me and one other on art, and one on music). We placed 5th overall and 2nd in visuals, sound, and theme! The final result is a RAGE 2D platformer where you break your PC to break the game. Turn up the brightness or smash in your screen to affect the levels and help your creature escape! Play the game here!
