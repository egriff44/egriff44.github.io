making almost anything



description


A grad course at MIT all about digital fabrication and bringing anything you can imagine to life! Makerspaces were utilized heavily to take on weekly design challenges and to incorporate a growing toolkit of fabrication techniques. Final projects utilized all techniques covered! Mine was a self teaching guitar, including a CAD-designed and laser cut guitar body with a custom LED fretboard and wifi-connected web app. New guitarists could utilize the app to be guided through chord and songs. Selecting an option on the app would light the corresponding fret/string on the fret board, making it easier to learn and to play along.
