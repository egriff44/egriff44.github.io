making almost anything



description


A grad course at MIT all about digital fabrication and bringing anything you can imagine to life! Makerspaces were utilized heavily to take on weekly design challenges that required incorporating a growing toolkit of fabrication techniques. Final projects used all techniques covered. Mine was a self teaching guitar - including a CAD-designed and laser-cut guitar body with a custom LED fretboard and wifi-connected web app. The app guides new guitarists through chords and songs. Selecting a chord/song option on the app would light the corresponding fret/string on the fret board, making it easier to learn and to play along.
