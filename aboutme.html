where i came from



My name is Emma - originally from Massachusetts and now living in Denver, CO!

I've always loved learning, and I've always had interests spanning disciplines from science and technology to art and design. Going into college, it was very important to me to develop a skillset fit for a well-rounded engineer and one that would allow me to bring imagined projects to life. This led me to MIT. I graduated in 2021 with a BS in Materials Science and Engineering and a concentration in Chinese. Throughout my time there, I was able to gain and develop skills in not just material science but also in areas such as software and hardware engineering, product design and fabrication, human computer interaction, robotics, and immersive technology!
